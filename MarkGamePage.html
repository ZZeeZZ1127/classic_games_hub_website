tic tac toe

Last Update: 6/8/2025

Lastest Version: alpha

Mark Liu

I created this Gomoku game, it's like a bigger version of tic-tac-toe, you need to place the piece five in a row to win. I make this because my friends and I often play it in our school’s Chinese Chess Club. However, we always needed a physical board, which made it hard to play outside the club. That’s why I decided to make a digital version that we can play anytime on the computer.
